About

MyMET Recommends is a gallery of artworks from the Metropolitan Museum based on what its online users are most interested in. It allows you to create your own selection of works and find out where they're located in the museum, so you can better plan your next visit.

How it Works

Through MyMET, MET users can make and keep their own art collections. MyMET Recommends compares shared items accross those collections to build a gallery of similar artworks. In other words, it is a lot similar to the recommendations Amazon does with its "Customers Who Viewed this Also Bought...." feature.
